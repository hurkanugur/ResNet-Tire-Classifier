Dataset Download Instructions

1. Download the dataset from Google Drive:
https://drive.google.com/file/d/1_sR9tTuYAGgjiPNx9AelcsG8IStg6F1j/view?usp=sharing

2. Unzip the downloaded file (data.zip) into your project directory.

3. Ensure the extracted "data" folder is at the same level as your "src" and "model" directories
